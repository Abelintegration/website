Enterprise Architecture

Enterprise Architecture considers the enterprise as a system where information flows between groups, departments, divisions, or subsidiary companies, with the intent of providing the customer a timely and unique product or service.

Introduction

Abel Integration is a site that promotes Enterprise Architecture and Enterprise Technical Architecture as disciplines that are essential to growing and streamlining any organization in today’s competitive business landscape.

Enterprise Technical Architecture

Enterprise Technical Architecture (ETA) defines the technologies that are used to build the IT environment that supports the Enterprise Architecture. It also defines the controls that maintain the business segmentation as defined by the Enterprise Architecture.
